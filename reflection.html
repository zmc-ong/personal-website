Reflection

I've learned a lot this term.

I was able to learn new coding languages while brushing up on things I was already familiar with. It's interesting to see how despite their differences in syntax, most programming languages are similar in nature.

Being able to experiment with code is still as fun as I remember, and the experience gained will be invaluable in the future. Although I'm not entirely optimistic about my future prospects, I have hope that it'll be okay eventually.

Perhaps delving deeper into my passions is the first step to blossoming into who I'm meant to be. And perhaps it's just another door that might close before I fully explore it. That's the uncertainty of what's to come.

But I believe I can do it. Change comes slowly, and it doesn't always feel good, but the end result will be worth it.
